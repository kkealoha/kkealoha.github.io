NYC Food Club

Changing the way you shop, changing the way you eat, changing the way you live.

Bringing bulk food buying clubs to the NYC areas that need them most.

Bulk food buying clubs (BFBCs) bring together members of a coumminty with a common goal of improving access to a variety of top-quality groceries at an affodrable price. Ability to purschase nutritious foods is especially important in urban areas where healthy options are limited and commutes to the better-stocked stores aren't always feasible.

How does it work?

Buying food in bulk drastically cuts the costs of grocery shopping. Once a community club is formed, NYC Food Club will connect connect club members with partner farmers and vendors. A catalog designed by NYC Food Club will include staple items as well as products catering to expressed desires of the communiy. Weekly orders will be delivered to a central location on Saturday, where payments can then be made.

We accept SNAP/EBT

Your paycheck should never get in the way between you and a healthful diet.

Check out our Base Catalog

NYC Food Club features a basic catalog, though full catalogs will vary between communitiy clubs depneding on each communities' needs.

Volunteer subsidies

Club members who are willing to volunteer their time to picking up and delivering orders, as well as to overseeing delivery distribution will have their orders subsidized accordingly.

Relationship between nutrition and health

We update our site and newsletters with news, facts, and statistics on the impact of diet on health and quality of life.
